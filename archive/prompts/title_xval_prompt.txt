- has_colon: the title uses a colon to separate a main title from a subtitle (do NOT count a ratio like '2:1' or a time like '10:00').

- is_versus: the title presents a HEAD-TO-HEAD comparison of two named interventions/things (e.g. 'A versus B', 'A vs B', 'A compared with B').
- is_review_meta: the work is a systematic review and/or meta-analysis / pooled analysis.
- is_case: the title is a case report or case series (a single/few-patient clinical case), EVEN IF it does not literally say 'case report'.
